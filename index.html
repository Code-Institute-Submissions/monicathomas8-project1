What is Meditation

Meditation is a practice of being still in body and mind with awareness of one self. Meditation has been around for years, the 5th Century B.C in fact! It plays a vital part in many cultures around the world. There are many types of meditation to practice and ponder over, each method is unique and carries many different elements. The one thing they all have in common is that they all focus on connecting the body and mind together. One of the most common types is Mindfulness Meditation, which was created by Siddhartha Gautama AKA Budda! Meditation can easily be incorporated into any lifestyle, everyone and anyone can do it.

Why Meditate

Stressed, need to relax, feeling overwhelmed?
Meditation can help defuse these feelings and bring a realm of calm and clarity into your life.

Meditation is known to help relieve:

  • Stress
  • Brain fog
  • Feelings of being overwhelmed
  • Feeling tired
  • Lack of focus
  • Lack of energy
When practiced on a regular basis you can create:
  • A sense of calm in yourself and your life
  • Self-control
  • Become less reactive to things that happen to you
  • More awareness for yourself and others around you
  • More thoughtful in your actions
